Solar Powered Engineering

Harnessing sunlight for sustainable transportation

Project Overview

Our solar powered car converts sunlight directly into electrical energy using photovoltaic panels. The generated energy powers a DC motor that drives the vehicle forward.

Engineering Design

We test gear ratios, motor configurations, and panel placements to maximize speed and efficiency. Structural balance and weight distribution are key for optimal performance.

Testing & Performance

Performance is measured by analyzing speed, torque, and energy output under varying sunlight intensities to improve efficiency.
