Solar Powered Car Project

Project Overview

Our solar powered car converts sunlight into electrical energy using a solar panel. The energy powers a DC motor that drives the wheels. This project explores renewable transportation and sustainable engineering design.

Engineering Design

We are testing different gear ratios, motor configurations, and solar panel placements to maximize speed and efficiency. Weight distribution and friction reduction are also key design considerations.

Testing & Performance

Performance is evaluated by measuring speed, torque, and energy output under various sunlight conditions. These tests help us optimize the system for real-world efficiency.
