Solar Powered Car Project

Harnessing Solar Energy for Transportation

Project Overview

Our solar powered car uses a solar panel to convert sunlight directly into electrical energy to power a motor. This project explores renewable transportation systems and sustainable vehicle engineering.

Engineering Design

We are testing motor configurations, gear ratios, and panel placement to maximize speed and efficiency under various light conditions.

Testing & Performance

Performance is measured by analyzing speed, torque, and energy output under different sunlight intensities.
