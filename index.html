Windmill Engineering Project

Designing and building a renewable energy system powered by wind and solar innovation.

Project Overview

This project focuses on creating a wind and solar powered vehicle that demonstrates renewable energy efficiency. Our goal is to explore sustainable engineering solutions and understand how clean energy can power transportation.

Engineering Design

The engineering process includes research, prototyping, aerodynamic testing, and system optimization. We are designing turbine blades to maximize airflow efficiency and integrating solar panels to provide supplemental power.

Project Progress

We are currently developing our blade prototypes and refining the wind energy capture system. Testing and improvements are ongoing as we work toward building a fully functional renewable energy vehicle. Photos and detailed documentation will be added soon.
