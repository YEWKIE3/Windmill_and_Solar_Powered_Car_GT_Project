Windmill Engineering Project

Innovating Renewable Energy Through Design

Project Overview

Our windmill project focuses on designing and constructing a functional small-scale wind turbine capable of converting kinetic wind energy into electrical energy.

Through research, prototyping, and experimentation, we are analyzing how blade angle, blade shape, material selection, and wind speed affect power output.

Engineering Design

We are testing different blade designs, materials, and structural layouts to maximize efficiency and durability.

Solar Powered Car

In addition to our windmill project, we are designing and building a solar powered car that converts sunlight into electrical energy using a solar panel.

The vehicle uses a DC motor powered directly by solar energy. We are testing different gear ratios, motor configurations, and weight distribution to maximize speed and efficiency.

This project allows us to explore renewable transportation technology and understand how solar energy can be applied to real-world engineering systems.
